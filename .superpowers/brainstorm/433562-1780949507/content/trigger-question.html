Where should "link as transfer" live?

Currently you click a "Link" button on the category cell of a non-transfer row to open the link modal. The question is: should linking also be triggerable from inside the inline row editor?

Current flow
Category Outflow
⇄ Transfer €42.00
🔴 Groceries €18.50

→ Click "Link" to open a 2-step modal (pick account, then pick matching transaction)

What would change

When you click a row to edit it inline, the category field would gain a "Transfer to…" option at the top of the dropdown:

€18.50

→ Choosing "Transfer to account…" opens the same 2-step link flow inline
