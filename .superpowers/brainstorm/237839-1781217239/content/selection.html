A — Click to select (no checkboxes)

Click any category row or group header to select it. Rows highlight with a green tint. Click again to deselect. Clean, no extra columns.

B — Checkboxes

A checkbox column on every row. Group checkbox checks/unchecks all categories in that group. More explicit but adds visual noise to the table.

I'd lean toward Option A — it keeps the table clean and the interaction is still intuitive. Checkboxes add a column that's always visible even when you don't need selection.
