How should income categories relate to "Expected Income"?

The budget header currently has a single "Expected Income" field. With Paychecks / Interest / Other Income added as budget categories, how should they interact?

A

Independent

Income categories show planned/actual per source. The "Expected Income" header field stays separate — you still type the overall total there manually.

Two separate concepts: header total vs. per-category breakdown.

B

Replace

Income category planned amounts become your expected income. The "Expected Income" input disappears — you budget income by category only.

Cleaner, but you must always budget each income source explicitly.

C

Auto-sum

Income category planned amounts automatically add up into "Expected Income". Budget $3,000 Paychecks + $200 Interest → Expected Income shows $3,200 automatically.

Most integrated — one source of truth.
